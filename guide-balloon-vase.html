The Steps — At a Glance

1
Inflate & prepare the balloonInflate to 60–70% only — not fully tight. Place upright in a small bowl or ring. Spray lightly with cooking spray for easy release later.
2
Mix & pour the concreteMix to a smooth, pourable cream consistency. Use a wide funnel and pour in stages — 1/4 at a time — tapping the balloon gently between each pour to settle.
3
Cure upside downFlip so the knot faces down and sits in a cup or ring. This creates a natural flat base. Cover loosely and cure 48–72 hours without moving.
4
Pop, peel & sandPop with a pin and peel the balloon away. Sand with 120 grit then 220 grit until silky smooth. Wipe clean and let dry fully before painting.
5
Paint gold interior & sealApply 2–3 thin coats of gold paint inside with a foam brush. Once dry, seal the entire exterior with matte acrylic sealer. Add pampas grass and display! ✨

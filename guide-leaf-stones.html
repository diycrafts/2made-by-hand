The Steps — At a Glance

1
Choose & prepare your leafPick a large, fresh leaf with prominent veins. Lay vein-side up on a flat surface. Coat the vein side lightly with cooking spray.
2
Mix the concreteMix to a thick, smooth consistency — like thick yogurt. Pour into a round mold or free-form on plastic sheeting.
3
Press the leafPress the leaf vein-side down firmly into the concrete. Smooth the back and let it sit undisturbed for 20 minutes.
4
Cure for 24–48 hoursCover with plastic and leave in shade. The longer you wait, the stronger and sharper the imprint will be.
5
Peel & finishCarefully peel the leaf away — the veins are now permanently cast! Sand edges smooth and seal. Install in your garden path. 🌿

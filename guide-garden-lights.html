The Process

How it all comes together

01

Waterproof Your LED

Before anything touches concrete, seal the LED puck. The guide shows the exact method — takes 2 minutes and ensures it works for years outdoors.

02

Prepare the Mold

A simple round mold gives the classic smooth stone shape. The guide covers how to oil it so your light pops out cleanly every time.

03

Pour & Set the LED

Pour the first layer, place the LED face-down in the center, then fill around it. The positioning trick ensures even light diffusion across the whole surface.

04

Cure for 24 Hours

Cover and leave it. The guide tells you exactly how long to wait and how to test if it is ready to unmold without cracking.

05

Unmold & Sand

Pop it out, smooth rough edges with sandpaper, apply a light sealant coat. The whole finishing process takes under 10 minutes.

06

Place & Switch On

Set them along your garden path, click the LED on, and watch your backyard transform. Each one takes under 30 minutes active time to make.

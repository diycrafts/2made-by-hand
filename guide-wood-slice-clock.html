The Process

How it all comes together

01

Dry & Assess Your Wood Slice

Your slice must be bone dry before anything else. Press a paper towel on the surface — any moisture transfer means more drying time. Hairline cracks are fine. Deep splits need wood glue and clamps first.

02

Sand to a Smooth Finish

Work through 80 → 120 → 220 → 400 grit, always following the grain. Wipe between each grit. Sand the edges and back too — people notice when the clock is on the wall.

03

Drill the Center Hole

Find the true center using the paper folding method in the guide — works on any irregular shape. Use a 10–12mm spade bit, drill from the front face downward.

04

Oil & Seal the Wood

Apply Danish oil following the grain. Generous first coat, soak 15 minutes, wipe off excess. Two coats total. This is the step that makes the grain come alive.

05

Mark Hours & Install Movement

Use the paper circle folding method to mark 12 equal positions. Gold paint pen for each marker. Thread mechanism through, attach hands, insert battery.

06

Add LED Backlight & Hang

Press a 30cm warm white LED strip around the back edge. Attach bracket, mount on wall, switch on the backlight for the jaw-dropping glow effect.

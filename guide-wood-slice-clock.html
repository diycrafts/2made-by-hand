The Process

How it all comes together

01

Prepare & Dry the Wood Slice

The guide shows how to check if your slice is dry enough and how to speed-dry it safely if needed — skipping this step is the #1 reason clocks crack.

02

Sand Through the Grits

Start coarse, finish fine. The guide covers the exact sandpaper sequence and the trick for getting that mirror-smooth finish without flattening the natural texture.

03

Drill the Center Hole

One hole, perfectly centered. The guide includes a foolproof marking method so you never drill off-center — even on irregular-shaped slices.

04

Oil & Seal the Wood

Apply the finish that brings the grain to life. The guide specifies exactly which oil, how many coats, and how long to wait between them.

05

Add Hour Marks & Install Mechanism

Mark the 12 hour positions perfectly using the guide's spacing method, paint them gold, then install the clock mechanism in under 5 minutes.

06

Mount & Style

Hang it on the wall and add the optional LED backlight. The guide covers mounting for plaster, drywall and brick — and how to hide the wire cleanly.

Process Preview

Steps Overview

01

Prepare Your Molds

Apply mold release and assemble multi-part silicone gnome molds for casting.

02

Mix the Concrete

Achieve perfect consistency — thick, lump-free, pourable but holding its shape.

03

Pour & Vibrate

Fill the mold and eliminate air bubbles for sharp facial and texture detail.

04

Wait for Green Stage

Let concrete reach the firm-but-workable stage before demolding and carving.

05

Sculpt & Carve Details

Use sculpting tools to refine beard, hat, glasses and facial features.

06

Add Color Inserts

Press tinted concrete into jeans, coat and hat areas for built-in color.

07

Wet Cure for 24–48h

Slow drying prevents cracking. Mist regularly for strong, durable concrete.

08

Sand & Refine

Remove seam lines and rough edges with 120 then 220-grit sandpaper.

09

Paint & Seal

Basecoat, dry-brush highlight, detail paint and exterior sealer for lasting finish.

10

Display in the Garden

Arrange your gnomes along paths, near flower beds or hidden in shrubs.

The Steps — At a Glance

1
Prepare the moldsSpray both buckets with cooking spray. Hot glue the rope start at the base of the outer bucket and wrap tightly upward, row by row, gluing every few rows to hold.
2
Insert the inner bucketCenter the smaller bucket inside the rope-wrapped outer bucket, leaving a 1.5–2 inch gap all around. Drop a few small rocks in the gap at the base for drainage.
3
Pour the concreteMix to thick yogurt consistency. Pour slowly into the gap between the two buckets, tapping the outer bucket on the table regularly to release air bubbles.
4
Cure for 48 hoursCover with plastic, keep in a cool shaded area, and mist lightly after 24 hours. Do not move or flex the mold during this time — patience makes it strong.
5
Demold & finishRemove the inner bucket by twisting, then peel or cut the outer bucket away. The rope texture is now in the concrete! Sand rough edges, seal, and plant. 🌿
